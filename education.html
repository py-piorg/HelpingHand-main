How it works

Helping The School

01

I. Technology Upgrade

The school urgently needs additional computers to modernize classrooms and provide students with essential tools for a comprehensive education.

02

II. Principal's Office Revitalization

The principal's office requires refurbishment, including a wardrobe, computer, and suitable furniture, to create a conducive work environment and efficiently manage crucial school and student information.

03

III. Teaching and Learning Resoruces

Academic enrichment necessitates the provision of essential teaching and learning resources such as charts, marker boards, and other aids to foster an interactive and engaging educational experience.

04

IV. Library Establishment

There is a proposal to establish a dedicated library as a central hub for students and teachers to support research and intellectual exploration, promoting a culture of continuous learning.

05

V. Infastructure Improvement

Beyond academics, attention is needed for the physical environment. Renovating the old block for teachers' accommodation and investing in a mower for regular compound maintenance are crucial for enhancing living conditions and creating a visually appealing learning environment.
