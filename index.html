Our Mission



Education.


We work to ensure that these children get the education they need and deserve.




Technology.


We work to give the school(Akeyase/Dida Methodist JHS) the resources and technology they need. The only technology the school currently has is a pair of keyboards.




Infastructure.


The school is rundown; since it is so far away from the city, it requires teachers to stay at the school. However, since the school buildings are crumbling, they cannot get enough teachers to help teach the students.
